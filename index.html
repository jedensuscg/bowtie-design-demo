OUR COMPANY

Is your company

We are Harry and Sally, a pair of Doctors who turned their love of bowties and passion for creating into a business. We started selling bowties locally, and became known for our quality and attention to detail. We eventually bought a wharehouse, and scaled our business to the largest custome bowtie botique on the web.

We handcraft every bowtie we ship. We personally vet and train every employer who works for us, so that each and every bowtie is crafted with the same combination of exacting standards and personal touch that you would recieve from us. Even Harry and Sally frequent still frequently create bowties for our customers.

We believe that there is no occasion where a bowtie can not be proudly worn. From a traditional black tie affair, or a saturday night at the club. We also believe that bowties are inherently personal, and that each bowtie should reflect the wearers personality and style. This is why we feel that the only way to craft a bowtie, is to craft it by hand, with input from the person who will end up wearing it. You tell us what you want, and we will handcraft it, for you and no one else. There are no robotic machines or bulk sewing machines in our wharehouse, only small, hand operated machines that your grandmother might have used.
